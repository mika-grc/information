About the School

Thomas O' Neill School was founded in 2004 by Dr. Erlinda P. Barcelo. The school is located in Barangay Talamban, Cebu City, Hi-way 77.

This aims to empower students to become curious, creative, and compassionate leaders who shape a brighter future for themselves and their communities.

In addition, the school provides a nurturing and inclusive learning environment that fosters academic excellence, critical thinking, and social responsibility.
